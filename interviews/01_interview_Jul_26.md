# Interview Retrospective #01
**Role:** LLM / Prompt Engineer  
**Date:** July 2026

## Objective

This retrospective documents the technical topics that were discussed during the interview, identifies knowledge gaps, and defines a concrete learning plan.

The purpose is not to evaluate the interview outcome, but to continuously improve my engineering skills.

---

# Overall Reflection

The interview confirmed that I have solid practical experience building LLM applications, including prompt engineering, LangChain, evaluation pipelines.

At the same time, it exposed several theoretical gaps in classical machine learning and the internal mechanics of large language models. These topics became the primary focus of my learning plan.
